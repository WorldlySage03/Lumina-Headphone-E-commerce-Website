Our Story

Combining aesthetics with audiophile perfection.

Born out of an obsession with sound.

At LUMINA, we believe that an audio device should be as beautifully crafted as the music it plays. Founded by a team of acoustic engineers and visionary industrial designers, we set out to build the definitive smart headphone.

We abandoned plastic in favor of aerospace-grade aluminum. We eschewed off-the-shelf drivers and engineered our own from the ground up. The result is the Lumina Studio Pro — a true masterpiece of sound and design.
